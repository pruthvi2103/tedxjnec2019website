How do I use it?

Number Entry

Simply type a number into either of the entry boxes and Phiculator will calculate the corresponding number according to the golden ratio.

Enter a number into the larger box at the top, to calculate the smaller proportional value in the box below, or vice-versa enter a number into the smaller box at the bottom, to calculate the larger proportional value in the box above. Confused? Oh come on, read all that again. Got it? Ok good.

Direction

Indicates which way you are performing the calculation, up or down the ratio, small to large or large to small.

Φ INT

Switch to Φ INT (phi, integer) mode to get your calculations as integers i.e. rounded to the nearest whole number, no decimals.

Φ

Switch back to Φ and they'll be calculated to 12 decimal places.

CB:AC

Resets your Phiculator to the default values,
